Understanding Departments

In Nigerian institutions, courses are grouped into four major faculties based on the subjects you studied in secondary school.

Science & Technology

For students who took Physics, Chemistry, Biology, or Further Math. Leads to careers like Doctors, Engineers, Programmers, and Pharmacists.

Commercial & Business

For students who took Accounting, Commerce, and Economics. Leads to careers like Bankers, Accountants, Marketers, and Business Managers.

Arts & Humanities

For students who took Literature, History, and Government. Leads to careers like Lawyers, Journalists, Actors, and Authors.

Social Sciences

For students interested in how society works. Overlaps with Commercial and Arts. Leads to careers like Psychologists, Diplomats, and Sociologists.
