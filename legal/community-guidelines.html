Welcome to the OAE (Oluwatosin Academy of Excellence) Community.

These Community Guidelines exist to help create a safe, respectful, educational, and productive environment for students, educators, parents, contributors, and visitors who interact with OAE through our website, social media pages, messaging platforms, discussion groups, comment sections, events, classes, and any other community spaces.

By participating in the OAE community, you agree to follow these Guidelines in addition to our Terms & Conditions and other applicable policies.

Our goal is simple:

To build a community where people can learn, grow, ask questions, share knowledge, and support one another respectfully.

1. OUR COMMUNITY VALUES

OAE is built on the following principles:

  • Respect
  • Honesty
  • Learning
  • Excellence
  • Responsibility
  • Inclusiveness
  • Academic Integrity
  • Safety

Every member is expected to contribute positively to these values.

2. BE RESPECTFUL

Treat all members with respect regardless of:

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Nationality
  • School
  • Academic performance
  • Religion
  • Background
  • Experience level
  • Opinions

Healthy disagreement is allowed.

Personal attacks are not.

You may challenge ideas, but do not attack people.

3. NO HARASSMENT OR BULLYING

The following behaviors are prohibited:

  • Bullying
  • Harassment
  • Intimidation
  • Threats
  • Mocking others
  • Name-calling
  • Public humiliation
  • Personal attacks
  • Targeted abuse
  • Cyberbullying

Any attempt to make another user feel unsafe may result in content removal or account restrictions.

4. NO HATEFUL CONTENT

OAE does not allow content that promotes:

  • Hatred
  • Discrimination
  • Violence
  • Extremism
  • Bigotry
  • Harm against individuals or groups

Content targeting people based on personal characteristics is not permitted.

5. KEEP DISCUSSIONS EDUCATIONAL

OAE is primarily an educational platform.

Community spaces should focus on:

  • Learning
  • Academic discussions
  • Educational news
  • School admissions
  • Examinations
  • Scholarships
  • Tutorials
  • Student development
  • Career guidance
  • Constructive conversations

Off-topic discussions may be limited, redirected, or removed.

6. NO SPAM

Spam is not allowed.

Examples include:

  • Repeated messages
  • Mass posting
  • Irrelevant promotions
  • Fake giveaways
  • Clickbait links
  • Excessive advertisements
  • Unsolicited marketing
  • Referral spam
  • Repetitive comments

Users who repeatedly spam may be restricted or removed.

7. NO MISLEADING INFORMATION

Accuracy matters.

Do not post:

  • Fake admission news
  • Fake examination updates
  • False school announcements
  • Misleading scholarship information
  • Fabricated results
  • Unverified rumors presented as facts

If you are unsure whether information is correct, clearly state that it is unconfirmed.

8. ACADEMIC INTEGRITY

OAE supports honest learning.

Do not:

  • Share examination malpractice materials
  • Promote cheating
  • Sell leaked examination questions
  • Encourage academic dishonesty
  • Request unauthorized access to examination systems
  • Share stolen academic materials

We encourage genuine learning and fair academic practices.

9. NO ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES

You may not use OAE to:

  • Violate laws
  • Promote illegal activities
  • Commit fraud
  • Impersonate others
  • Distribute malware
  • Share stolen content
  • Engage in scams

Content involving illegal activities may be removed immediately.

10. RESPECT PRIVACY

Protect your own privacy and the privacy of others.

Do not share:

  • Personal addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Private emails
  • Identification documents
  • Examination numbers
  • Financial information
  • Login credentials
  • Private conversations without permission

Always obtain consent before sharing someone else's information.

11. APPROPRIATE CONTENT ONLY

Content shared within OAE should remain appropriate for a broad educational audience.

Avoid:

  • Explicit sexual content
  • Graphic violence
  • Excessive profanity
  • Obscene materials
  • Disturbing images
  • Adult-only content

Educational discussions involving sensitive subjects should remain respectful and appropriate.

12. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Respect the work of others.

Do not:

  • Upload copyrighted materials without permission
  • Claim another person's work as your own
  • Remove author credits
  • Distribute pirated content
  • Share paid materials illegally

Always provide proper attribution where necessary.

13. RESPONSIBLE USE OF AI TOOLS

Where AI tools are used within OAE:

  • Do not present AI-generated information as verified fact without checking it
  • Clearly identify AI-generated content where appropriate
  • Do not use AI to deceive, scam, impersonate, or spread misinformation
  • Verify important educational information before sharing

14. REPORTING CONTENT

If you see content that violates these Guidelines, please report it.

Reports may include:

  • Harassment
  • Spam
  • Fake information
  • Copyright violations
  • Safety concerns
  • Fraudulent activity

We review reports in good faith and take appropriate action where necessary.

15. MODERATION

OAE reserves the right to:

  • Review content
  • Remove content
  • Edit content
  • Restrict visibility
  • Suspend accounts
  • Ban users
  • Close discussions
  • Limit participation

Moderation decisions are made to protect the community and platform integrity.

16. ENFORCEMENT ACTIONS

Violations may result in:

Level 1:

  • Warning
  • Content removal

Level 2:

  • Temporary restrictions
  • Reduced platform access

Level 3:

  • Temporary suspension

Level 4:

  • Permanent removal from the community

Serious violations may skip directly to higher enforcement levels.

17. APPEALS

If you believe a moderation action was made in error, you may contact OAE and request a review.

Submitting an appeal does not guarantee reversal of the decision.

18. CHANGES TO THESE GUIDELINES

OAE may update these Guidelines from time to time.

Changes become effective when published unless otherwise stated.

Continued participation in OAE means acceptance of the updated Guidelines.

19. OUR COMMUNITY COMMITMENT

We believe education thrives when people can:

  • Learn freely
  • Share respectfully
  • Ask questions confidently
  • Support one another
  • Grow together

Every member helps shape the OAE community.
