This Cookie Policy explains how OAE (Oluwatosin Academy of Excellence) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit or interact with our website and services.

This Cookie Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

Cookies help websites:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Improve functionality
  • Analyze traffic
  • Maintain security
  • Deliver personalized experiences
  • Support advertising and analytics

Cookies do not usually contain information that directly identifies an individual, but they may be linked to information we hold about you.

2. WHY OAE USES COOKIES

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Operate the website
  • Improve website performance
  • Remember user settings
  • Maintain login sessions
  • Analyze visitor behavior
  • Improve content delivery
  • Measure traffic and engagement
  • Detect fraud and abuse
  • Enhance security
  • Support advertising and sponsorship services

3. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

A. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of the website.

Examples include:

  • Login authentication
  • Session management
  • Security protection
  • Form submissions
  • Load balancing

Without these cookies, some services may not function properly.

B. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand:

  • How visitors use the website
  • Which pages are popular
  • How users navigate the site
  • Technical performance issues

Examples may include:

  • Google Analytics
  • Website traffic analysis tools
  • User engagement tracking

C. Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember:

  • Language preferences
  • Theme settings
  • Saved preferences
  • Customized experiences

D. Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure advertisement performance
  • Limit repetitive ads
  • Improve advertising effectiveness

Advertising partners may also place cookies on your device.

4. THIRD-PARTY COOKIES

Some cookies may be placed by third parties including:

  • Google
  • Meta (Facebook)
  • YouTube
  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics providers
  • Embedded content providers

These organizations may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

OAE does not control third-party cookies.

5. HOW LONG COOKIES REMAIN

Some cookies are:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when your browser closes
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or are manually removed

Retention periods vary depending on the specific cookie.

6. MANAGING COOKIES

Most browsers allow users to:

  • View cookies
  • Delete cookies
  • Block cookies
  • Restrict cookies
  • Receive notifications about cookies

Browser settings can usually be adjusted through:

  • Chrome settings
  • Firefox settings
  • Safari settings
  • Microsoft Edge settings
  • Mobile browser settings

Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

OAE may use advertising technologies that:

  • Deliver relevant advertisements

Advertising technologies may involve cookies, pixels, tags, or similar tools.

You may withdraw consent at any time through available settings or tools.

9. SECURITY AND COOKIES
