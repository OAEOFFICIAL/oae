Current JAMB Syllabus for All Subjects

The JAMB Syllabus is the official guide provided by the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) that outlines all the topics and areas you are expected to study for your UTME (Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination).

This syllabus serves as a roadmap to help candidates focus on relevant areas while preparing for the exam. Whether you are taking JAMB for the first time or retaking it, this syllabus ensures you are fully aware of the subjects and topics that may appear in your examination.

What is the JAMB Syllabus?

The JAMB Syllabus contains a detailed breakdown of topics for each subject examined in the UTME. It helps students understand the scope of the exams and plan their studies effectively.

Every question in the UTME is derived from the topics listed in this syllabus. Studying outside this syllabus is not necessary for UTME preparation. By following this guide, you can maximize your performance, reduce unnecessary stress, and target your preparation towards the most relevant areas.

In addition, the syllabus often includes examples, subtopics, and learning objectives to give candidates a clear idea of what they are expected to know.

How to Use the JAMB Syllabus Effectively

  • Read each subject's syllabus carefully. Take time to understand the scope and objectives outlined for each subject you're taking.
  • Identify the topics you are less confident in and prioritize them. Focus your study time on areas where you need improvement.
  • Use past questions to practice questions from each syllabus topic. This helps you understand how JAMB tests these topics.
  • Plan a study schedule according to the subjects and topics outlined in the syllabus. Create a realistic timetable that covers all topics.
  • Remember that consistent revision is key. Regular review of topics ensures better retention and understanding.
