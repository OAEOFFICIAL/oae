Section Topics / Focus Details / Objectives Recommended Texts OAE Notes
1
Drama		 Types: Tragedy, Comedy, Tragicomedy, Melodrama, Farce, Opera
Techniques: Characterization, Dialogue, Soliloquy, Stagecraft		 Interpret themes, plot, setting and socio-political context; analyze dramatic techniques and compare playwright styles. Soyinka (plays), Osborne (Look Back in Anger), Critical texts (Abrams) Check OAE notes
2
Prose		 Types: Novel, Novella, Short story, Biography, Memoir, Faction
Techniques: POV, Characterization, Narrative structure		 Differentiate prose types, analyze narrative techniques and relate texts to real-life issues. Emecheta, Brontë (Wuthering Heights), Selected African prose collections Check OAE notes
3
Poetry		 Forms: Sonnet, Ode, Lyric, Elegy, Ballad, Epic, Blank verse
Devices: Imagery, Sound, Diction, Persona		 Identify poetic forms, analyze devices, interpret themes and socio-political relevance. Senghor, Osundare, Donne, Angelou, Eliot Check OAE notes
4
General Literary Principles		 Foreshadowing, Symbolism, Satire, Stream of Consciousness, Monologue, Dialogue Identify and apply literary terms; analyze their function in texts. Abrams (A Glossary of Literary Terms), Nwoga (West African Verse) Check OAE notes
5
Literary Appreciation		 Unseen extracts from Drama, Prose and Poetry Identify devices, interpret meanings and relate to real-life applications. Murphy (Understanding Unseen), Anthologies listed below Check OAE notes

Selected Prescribed Texts

Drama: Wole Soyinka — Lion and the Jewel; John Osborne — Look Back in Anger

Prose: Buchi Emecheta — Second Class Citizen; Alex Agyei Agyiri — Unexpected Joy at Dawn; Emily Brontë — Wuthering Heights

Poetry: See recommended anthology list below (Senghor, Osundare, Maya Angelou, Donne, Eliot etc.)

Recommended Texts

  • Gbemisola A. — Naked Soles
  • Hayward J. (ed.) — The Penguin Book of English Verse
  • Johnson R. et al. (eds.) — New Poetry from Africa
  • Kermode F. et al. — Oxford Anthology of English Literature Vol. II
  • Nwoga D. (ed.) — West African Verse
  • Parker E. W. (ed.) — A Pageant of Longer Poems
  • Senanu K. E. & Vincent T. (eds.) — A Selection of African Poetry
  • Soyinka W. (ed.) — Poems of Black Africa
  • Abrams M. H. — A Glossary of Literary Terms
  • Murphy M. J. — Understanding Unseen: An Introduction to English Poetry and Novel
