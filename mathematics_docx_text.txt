Question 1

Which of the following points does not lie on the line \(2y + 5x - 4 = 0\)?
Options:
(a) (0.8, 0)
(b) (1, -0.5)
(c) (0, 2)
(d) (2, 3)
Correct Answer: (d) (2, 3)
Explanation: A point lies on the line if its coordinates satisfy the equation. Substitute each point into \(2y + 5x - 4 = 0\).
For (a) (0.8, 0): \(2(0) + 5(0.8) - 4 = 0 + 4 - 4 = 0\) ✓ lies on line.
For (b) (1, -0.5): \(2(-0.5) + 5(1) - 4 = -1 + 5 - 4 = 0\) ✓ lies on line.
For (c) (0, 2): \(2(2) + 5(0) - 4 = 4 + 0 - 4 = 0\) ✓ lies on line.
For (d) (2, 3): \(2(3) + 5(2) - 4 = 6 + 10 - 4 = 12\), not 0. So (2,3) does not lie on the line. Answer is (d).

(b) The gradient is 3 and the y-intercept is 1.5
(c) The gradient is 15 and the y-intercept is 3

Correct Answer: (a) The gradient is 0.3 and the y-intercept is 1.5

So gradient (m) = 0.3, y-intercept (c) = 1.5. Option (a) matches.

Options:
(a) 1
(b) -1
(c) 0.5
(d) -0.5
Correct Answer: (b) -1
Explanation: Gradient = (change in y) / (change in x) = (y₂ - y₁) / (x₂ - x₁).

So gradient = (3 - 7) / (6 - 2) = (-4) / (4) = -1. Answer is (b).
