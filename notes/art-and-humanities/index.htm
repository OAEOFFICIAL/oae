What is Art & Humanities?

Art & Humanities encompasses the study of human culture, creativity, expression, and society. This discipline includes the study of languages, literature, history, philosophy, religious studies, visual arts, music, and social sciences. These subjects develop critical thinking, communication skills, and cultural awareness essential for understanding our world.

Whether exploring written masterpieces, analyzing historical events, creating visual art, or understanding societal structures, Art & Humanities disciplines provide insights into the human experience and prepare students for careers in education, media, diplomacy, culture, and social sciences.
