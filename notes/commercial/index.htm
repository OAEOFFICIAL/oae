What is Commercial Studies?

Commercial Studies encompasses the discipline of business management, accounting, finance, economics, and office administration. These subjects prepare students for careers in accounting, business management, commerce, finance, entrepreneurship, and trade.

Whether pursuing accounting qualifications, business management roles, or entrepreneurial ventures, Commercial Studies disciplines provide the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the modern business world and prepare for professional success.
