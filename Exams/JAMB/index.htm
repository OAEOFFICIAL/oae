What is JAMB UTME?

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) is Nigeria's standardized university entrance exam. It evaluates candidates in four subjects and is mandatory for admission into any Nigerian university.
