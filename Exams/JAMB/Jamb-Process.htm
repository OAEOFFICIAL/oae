JAMB Registration Process

Complete step-by-step guide to registering for JAMB UTME

1

Create JAMB Profile

Visit the JAMB website and create your profile using your valid email address and phone number. You'll receive a confirmation code.

2

Generate Profile Code

Send NIN [space] YOUR NAME to 55019 from the phone number you used in registration to generate your profile code.

Tip: Make sure to use the exact name on your NIN slip.
3

Purchase UTME Form

Visit any authorized JAMB CBT center or bank to purchase your UTME form using your profile code.

4

Complete Registration

Return to the CBT center with your profile code and required documents to complete your registration and capture your biometrics.

Required Documents:
  • Valid Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • NIN
  • O'Level Results (if available)
  • Valid Means of Identification
  • 4 Passport Photographs
5

Print Examination Slip

Two weeks before your exam, return to the JAMB website to print your examination slip which contains your exam center and time.
